FLAPPY BIRDS

ABOUT THE GAME:

How to play

This game is played only one key that is the space key, which is used to give the bird an upward momentum. Use Numpad 0 to turn 'ON' or 'OFF' the autoplay.

Objective

The objective of this game is to dodge the obstacles coming in the way of the bird. You have to fly in between the two pipes and touching any of them will cause the death of the bird.

Obstacles

The obstacles are blitted over the screen with a chaning x-axis. These obstacles come towards the player giving an illusion of the flying through the pipes. The distance between the upper pipe and the lower pipe is predefined and it is the minimum distance possible allowed for the bird to fly. Heights of these obstacles are choosen at random using the random library.

The auto play function checks if the obstacle is lower or above the bird and hence passes out the command if the birds will fly upwards or glide down. It returns true if it wants the bird to fly and false otherwise.
